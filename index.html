    CONTRACT SDK

    • Distribution contract

      Developers, guilds and players are distributed with rewards based on the yields of the game and the contract rules

    • Trading contract

      Developers have access to the trading contracts pre-written in the games, and easily receive a commission of the transaction fees generated from in-game assests trading.

    • Staking & leasing contract

      Developers and guilds can stake and lease their NFT, players can also stake and lease their virtual assets in the game, to obtain income.

    • Cross-chain token swapping contract

      StoreFi provides cross-chain token swapping contract, where initially 12 token pairs are supported, on top of all direct swap between game tokens and the platform token SF.

    • Voting contract

      The voting contract regulates all votings that the token-holders take, at times when rules require upgrades and updates, for the good of the platform development.
